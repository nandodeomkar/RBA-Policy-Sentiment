What these scores mean

Each decision statement is scored on a dovish ↔ hawkish scale (−1 to +1), with separate inflation, growth and employment sub-scores. A confidence signal reflects how much the scoring methods agree.

This is general information, not financial advice.

How it's built

An offline batch job scores each statement with a reconciled ensemble and publishes the result with its full breakdown. Engine —.

Scores are computed once and reproducible from the pinned engine version.
